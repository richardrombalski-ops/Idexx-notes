# Strategy for Accounts with Total Balance Less than $50,000

| Days Past Due | Stage | Due Date | Contact Type | Description |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Reminder | None | CAG | Invoices due on the 25th day of the following month |
| >4 | Reminder | Fax/Email | Communication to customer advising that payment was due on 9/25/20XX, explaining that they should contact us to make payment |
| | | Call if no fax | |  |
| 2-7 (with notation "12") | Escalated Serious | Fax/Email | Communication to customer advising that payment is past due, explaining that they should contact us to make payment |
| | | Call if no fax | |  |
| 2-8 (with notation "19") | Problem Serious | Fax/Email | Communication to customer advising that payment is delinquent, explaining that they should contact us to make payment. Advising of order hold placed if account reaches 30 days overdue. |
| | | Call if no fax | |  |
| ≥30 (with notation "231") | Order Hold | Fax/Email | Communication to customer advising that order placed if account and what the impact, how to rectify, etc. Can still place consumable orders with a credit card, warns that reference labs and telemedicine services may be impacted if not paid in timely manner. |
| | | Call if no fax | |  |
| ≥40 | Lab/Telemed Suspension Pending | Email to Sales | Contact field sales team to advise that the account is pending suspension of reference laboratory and/or telemedicine services. Send to RM, VDC, VDS, DR, VSS, and VSS Manager. This is sales' chance to weigh in and let us know better contact information, extenuating circumstances, etc. |
| ≥42 | Lab/Telemed Suspension | Call | a. We do not suspend reference laboratory/courier service on Fridays or last business day of a week. b. Call to inform the customer that their reference laboratory/courier and telemedicine services are going on hold today and remain on hold until the overdue balance has been resolved or we have a reasonable payment plan in place. c. Notify the transportation liaisons/supervisors and telemedicine accounts to block in applicable systems |
| ≥54 | Request to Avoid Further Demand | Letter | a. This is a physical letter we mail to the customer. b. The verbiage of this letter is as follows: "This notice will serve as a FINAL DEMAND. To date, all attempts to collect this debt have been unsuccessful. Unless we receive 50___ ] by [14 days from DATE of letter], we will be forced to place your account with an agency and/or attorney for collection. If you do not wish this action, please contact me directly. This will be our last request for your cooperation. It is our sincere hope that you will respond promptly to avoid further collection action. If payment has already been made since [date of letter], please accept our sincere thanks and disregard this notice." |
| ≥69 | 3rd Party Collections | A/R Form and Email | Credit Specialists write off the balance and send to our 3rd Party Collection Agency. |
