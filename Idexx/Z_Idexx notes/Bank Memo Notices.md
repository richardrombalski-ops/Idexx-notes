# Thursday and Fridays

## Bank Memo Notices — ACH's That Didn't Clear

### Procedure

1. Enter account number in **GetPaid**
2. Check notes in **GetPaid**
3. Highlight row → **Copy**
4. Right-click → **Send**
5. Dropdown → **Email / Fax**
6. Select **Bank Memo Notice**
7. Click **Edit and Send**
8. Paste Bank Memo in blank spot in email
9. If **Red**, then look up in **Salesforce**
10. **Send**
