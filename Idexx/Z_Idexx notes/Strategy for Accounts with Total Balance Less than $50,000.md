## Strategy for Accounts with Total Balance Less than $50,000

| Days Past Due | Stage                          | Contact Type             | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
| ------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0             | Due Date                       | None                     | CAG invoices due on the 25th day of the following month                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| >4            | Rollout                        | Fax/Email                | Communication to customer advising that payment is now due on 00/25/XXXX, explaining that they should contact us to make payment                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| >12           | Problem                        | Call if no fax           | Communication to customer advising that payment is delinquent, explaining that they should contact us to make payment. Advising of order hold being placed if account reaches 30 days overdue.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| >19           | Serious                        | Call if no fax           | Communication to customer advising that payment is delinquent.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| >31           | Order Hold                     | Fax/Email Call if no fax | Communication to customer advising that order hold has been placed on account and that means, how to remedy, etc. Can still place orders with a credit card, warns that reference laboratory and telemedicine services may be impacted in a timely manner.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| >31           | Lab/Telemed Suspension Pending | Email to Sales           | Contact field sales team to inform that the account is pending suspension of reference laboratory and/or telemedicine services. If resolution is not reached regarding outstanding balance, send to RM, VOC, VDS, DR, VSS, and VSS Manager. This is sales' chance to evaluate circumstances, and let us know better contact information, extenuating circumstances, etc.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| >42           | Lab/Telemed Suspension         | Call                     | a. We do not suspend reference laboratory/courier service on Fridays or last business day of a week. b. Call to inform the customer that their reference laboratory/courier and telemedicine services are going on a hold today and services with telemedicine will not be resumed until the overdue balance has been resolved or we have a reasonable payment plan in place. c. Notify the transportation liaisons/supervisors and telemedicine accounts to block in applicable systems                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| >54           | Request to send Final Demand   | Letter                   | a. This is a physical letter we mail to the customer. b. The verbiage of this letter is as follows: "This notice will serve as a FINAL DEMAND. To date, all attempts to collect this debt have been unsuccessful. Unless we receive $[___] by [14 days from DATE of letter], we will be forced to place your account with an agency and/or attorney for collection. If you wish to avoid this action, please contact me directly. This will be our last request for your cooperation. It is our sincere hope that you will respond promptly to avoid further collection action. If payment has already been made since [date of letter], please accept our sincere thanks and disregard this notice." |
| ≥69           | 3rd Party Collections          | A/R Form and Email       | Credit Specialists write off the balance and send to our 3rd Party Collection Agency.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
